## Video Analysis Query Templates

### Temporal Specifications
Each query can accept one of these temporal parameters:
- whole_video: Analyze the entire video
- time_range: (start_time, end_time) in seconds
- timestamp: Specific time in seconds

### Plot Analysis
- describe_plot(time_range=None): "Describe the plot/narrative of the video."
- compare_scenes(entity1, entity2, time_range=None): "Compare scenes or actions between {entity1} and {entity2}."
- metaphor_analysis(time_range=None): "Identify metaphors or symbols in the scene."
- narrative_explanation(element, time_range=None): "Explain the narrative purpose of {element} in the scene."
- plot_theme(time_range=None): "Identify the main theme or message of the video."

### Element Counting
- count_elements(element_type, time_range=None): "Count the number of {element_type} in the video."
- count_events(event_type, time_range=None): "Count how many times {event_type} occurs."
- count_attempts(action, character=None, time_range=None): "Count how many attempts at {action} by {character}."

### Element Attributes
- describe_attribute(element, attribute=None, time_range=None): "Describe the {attribute} of {element}."
- identify_material(object, time_range=None): "Identify the material of {object}."
- identify_color(object, time_range=None): "Identify the color of {object}."
- identify_state(object, attribute=None, time_range=None): "Identify the state of {attribute} of {object}."
- optical_illusion_analysis(time_range=None): "Analyze the optical illusion present in the scene."

### Causality Analysis
- explain_causality(event, time_range=None): "Explain what caused {event} to happen."
- explain_videography_effect(effect, time_range=None): "Explain how the {effect} videography effect was achieved."
- explain_damage_cause(object, time_range=None): "Explain what caused damage to {object}."
- explain_appearance_change(object, time_range=None): "Explain how {object} appeared/disappeared/changed."
- explain_phenomenon(phenomenon, time_range=None): "Explain the {phenomenon} shown in the video."

### Professional Knowledge
- solve_math_problem(problem_index=None, time_range=None): "Solve the math problem shown in the video."
- explain_procedure(procedure, time_range=None): "Explain the procedure for {procedure} shown in the video."
- explain_technique(technique, time_range=None): "Explain the technique for {technique} demonstrated."
- assess_correctness(method, time_range=None): "Assess if {method} shown produces correct results."

### Event Localization
- localize_event(event, time_range=None): "Identify when {event} occurs in the video."
- identify_sequence(events, time_range=None): "Identify the sequence of {events}."
- identify_first_occurrence(event, time_range=None): "Identify the first occurrence of {event}."
- identify_last_occurrence(event, time_range=None): "Identify the last occurrence of {event}."
- localize_answer(question_index, time_range=None): "Identify when the answer to question {question_index} appears."

### Character Actions
- describe_action(character, time_range=None): "Describe what {character} is doing."
- compare_actions(character1, character2, time_range=None): "Compare the actions of {character1} and {character2}."
- explain_motivation(character, action=None, time_range=None): "Explain why {character} performed {action}."
- identify_purpose(action, character=None, time_range=None): "Identify the purpose of {action} by {character}."
- identify_winner(game=None, time_range=None): "Identify who won the {game}."

### Element Localization
- locate_element(element, time_range=None): "Locate {element} in the scene."
- describe_clothing(character, time_range=None): "Describe the clothing of {character}."
- identify_characteristics(person, time_range=None): "Identify the characteristics of {person}."
- identify_item_by_index(index, category=None, time_range=None): "Identify the {index}th item of {category} shown."
- identify_person_by_action(action, time_range=None): "Identify who performed {action}."

### Positional Relationships
- identify_hand_used(character, action, time_range=None): "Identify which hand {character} used for {action}."
- identify_position(object, reference=None, time_range=None): "Identify the position of {object} relative to {reference}."
- identify_body_part(character, action=None, time_range=None): "Identify which body part of {character} was involved in {action}."
- identify_direction(object, time_range=None): "Identify the direction that {object} is facing/moving."
- identify_landing_location(object, time_range=None): "Identify where {object} landed."

### Character Reactions
- describe_reaction(character, stimulus=None, time_range=None): "Describe {character}'s reaction to {stimulus}."
- explain_reaction_cause(character, reaction, time_range=None): "Explain what caused {character} to {reaction}."
- describe_emotion(character, time_range=None): "Describe the emotion of {character}."
- identify_mood(character, time_range=None): "Identify the mood of {character} and explain why."
- explain_surprise_cause(character=None, time_range=None): "Explain what surprised {character}."

### Movement and Speed
- compare_speed(entity1, entity2, time_range=None): "Compare the speed of {entity1} and {entity2}."
- identify_faster(entities, time_range=None): "Identify which of {entities} is faster."
- describe_displacement(object, time_range=None): "Describe the displacement of {object}."
- identify_direction(movement, object=None, time_range=None): "Identify the direction of {movement} for {object}."
- describe_motion(object, time_range=None): "Describe the motion of {object}."
- identify_rotation_direction(object, time_range=None): "Identify the rotation direction of {object}."

### Time-based Analysis
- measure_duration(event, time_range=None): "Measure the duration of {event}."
- compare_duration(event1, event2, time_range=None): "Compare the duration of {event1} and {event2}."
- describe_time_lapse(phenomenon, time_range=None): "Describe the time lapse of {phenomenon}."
- track_state_change(object, attribute=None, time_range=None): "Track how the state of {object}'s {attribute} changes over time."
